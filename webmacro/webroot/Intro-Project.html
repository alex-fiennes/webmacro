Open-Source Development

WebMacro is an open-source development project: When you download WebMacro, you get everything--all the documentation, all the sourcecode.

WebMacro is available under the GNU General Public License (GPL), as described in the license. Commercial users who cannot work with the GPL may purchase special commercial use licenses from Semiotek Inc., WebMacro's developers.

WebMacro is a group development effort.

We rely heavily on the WebMacro mailing list. There, features are discussed, votes are taken, and the future of WebMacro is decided.

Most of the important features and insights into WebMacro's design arose out of our mailing list. It's a great place to talk about features, get support, find out what other people are doing.

If you use WebMacro, or are interested in using it, or just have some ideas, please join the mailing list!
